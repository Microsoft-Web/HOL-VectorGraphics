Hey there, friendly face! Looks like you've found the face place; a place for faces just like yours!

Just place your face into the game window by dragging and dropping a picture of your most excellent mug or browsing your computer for the perfect pretty picture. A shot of you facing the camera is best; though don’t be afraid to start with something silly! Once your image has loaded on screen, you are ready to add some fun and funky features.

This is where you can get creative! Select a category from the panel above your picture. Choose your favorite silly face props and simply drag and drop them onto your face for a monster of a make-over. You can resize face props to fit your face or make them super big to complete your freakishly funny new look.

Once your masterpiece is finally complete, just click DONE and your awesome new work of art will be flattened into a bitmap which you can right click and save to your computer to share with your friends. You can save and return to a game at any time using The Face Place gallery. To start a fresh face just click NEW GAME or drag a thumbnail to the New Game area. Wasn’t that fun? We thought you might like it.
